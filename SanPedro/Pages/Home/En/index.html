A Guide to San Pedro de Atacama

Who Am I?

I was a research technician for the Cosmology Large Angular Scale Surveyor (CLASS) telescope living in San Pedro de Atacama. In my free time I liked to explore this beautiful area in my 4x4 trucks. I have visited all the major tour destinations and many other places as well! The popularity of San Pedro de Atacama has grown greatly as a tourist destination in the past years. As I met travelers who pass through San Pedro, I am often asked where to go and what to see. As an adventure loving travelers as well, I decided to put together this guide to offer a perspective to the area that you won't find in guide books! While I lived in San Pedro, I explored with my coworker Deniz. Currently he is still in San Pedro with his wife and we are all working together to keep this guide up to date!

-Manwei
