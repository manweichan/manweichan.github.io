My Work

A selection of projects I've worked on, beginning with the most recent. For more information, check out my CV

RSGS

I work on the guidance, navigation, and control team for the Robotic Servicing of Geosynchronous Satellites (RSGS) at Draper Labs. My research is determining a guidance algorithm that will allow the servicing satellite to dock with an uncontrolled tumbling client satellite.

CLASS

I worked on the Cosmology Large Angular Scale Surveyor (CLASS), a telescope studying the origins of our universe. Specifically, CLASS is looking for details in the Cosmic Microwave Background for signs of inflation. With CLASS, a prepared the 90GHz cryostat for deployment in the field and was later the senior technician at the site in Chile.
